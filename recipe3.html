Apple Spinach Salad



The addition of nuts and fruit makes this salad extra special.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1 medium apple
  • 2 tbsp. chopped celery
  • 2 tbsp. toasted PLANTERS Pecans, chopped
  • 2 tbsp. KRAFT LIGHT DONE RIGHT! House Italian Reduced Fat Dressing

Procedure

  1. To Toast Pecans:
    1. Spread nuts in a single layer in a shallow baking pan.
    2. Bake at 350 degrees, for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally
  2. Toss ingredients with dressing in large bowl.
  3. Serve! You can choose to make it ahead of time by covering and refrigerating the apples, celery, and dressing. Only toss with spinach and pecans just before serving.

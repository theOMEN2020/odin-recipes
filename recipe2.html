Greek Couscous Salad



This hearty Greek couscous salad uses Israeli couscous. So delicious!


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp. minced garlic
  • 1 cup pearl (Israeli) couscous
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced Kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp. crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp. white wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. ground black pepper

Procedure

  1. Pour water and chicken broth into a saucepan; stir in the garlic and bring to a boil.
  2. Stir in pearl couscous, cover the pan, and remove from heat.
  3. Allow couscous to stand until water has been absorbed, about 5 minutes; fluff with a fork. Allow couscous to cool to warm temperature.
  4. Lightly toss couscous, chickpeas, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese in a large serving bowl.
  5. To make the dressing: Mix white wine vinegar, lemon juice, oregano, and black pepper in a small bowl until well combined. Pour over couscous mixture; toss again to serve.

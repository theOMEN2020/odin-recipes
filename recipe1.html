Pecan Chicken Salad



This pecan chicken salad made with chopped cooked chicken, herbs, celery, pecans, red onion, and a creamy homemade dressing is really flavorful. Serve on top of toasted croissants or butter lettuce.


Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp. white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp. Park Hill maple pepper
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • 1/3 cup chopped toasted pecans
  • 2 tbsp. minced red onion

Procedure

  1. Mix mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, vinegar, garlic powder, thyme, and maple pepper together in a bowl until well combined.
  2. Add chicken, celery, pecans, and red onion; stir well to incorporate.
  3. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 3 days.
